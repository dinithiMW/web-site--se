Corporate Profile

SEL Engineering, an electrical engineering company established in 2000 by Mr. Amal karunarthna a well known character possessed with many years of experience in the electrical engineering business. Its business cultivation is with the policy of quality, safety, reliability and timely installation and completion of projects yield a splendid business harvest with in a very short period of time. It resulted in to a strong and long term relationship with customers and as a result SE continues to maintain the premier position in the electrical engineering sector. In addition the fame achieved by SE opened door to step in to the international market. Within a short span of time, we have captured a significant share in the domestic and industrial market due to our strong emphasis on high quality, competitive price and prompt delivery. The company is run by a team of trained professionals and a determined workforce who put immense efforts to achieve success by providing full satisfaction to our customers.

The Policy

SEL considers the electrical system to be an asset to last a lifetime and also the heart of the industry. Its policy is to ensure customer satisfaction as it does not compromise on the quality of material, workmanship, service and time. Quality Standards
